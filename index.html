Intro

This layout is designed to feel immersive and editorial. The home screen acts like a landing portal, while each section opens as an animated panel layered over the background.

It is ideal for portfolios, brand sites, teaser pages, creative studios, or personal profiles where you want a distinctive first impression without a traditional scrolling homepage.
